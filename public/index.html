About Me

I work at Uniswap Labs in Strategy and Operations. Uniswap Labs builds software that enables anyone to create and access markets, for any asset, at any time.

I’ve previously worked (reverse chronologically):

  • at Circle on public policy,
  • at the U.S. Consumer Financial Protection Bureau on payments and retail banking policy, and
  • for Congressman Jason Smith of Missouri.

I graduated cum laude from Claremont McKenna College, majoring in Philosophy, Politics, and Economics. I wrote my thesis on conceptions of property rights at the time of the American Founding.

Past Work - Professional

  • I helped construct the business strategy for Unichain, a new L2 designed for DeFi. I am a coauthor on the Unichain whitepaper. I explained Unichain at ETHDenver 2025.
  • I wrote Circle’s 2023 comment letter to the SEC on DeFi regulation, and I was the lead author on some other letters.
  • At the CFPB, I contributed to the Dodd-Frank Section 1033 ANPR and the Big Tech information orders.
  • At Claremont McKenna, I was one of four student contributors to the college's 2019 Strategy Committee, which developed and published a strategic review ahead of a new capital campaign that has raised over $1 billion.
